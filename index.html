Simon is an electronic game of memory skill invented by Ralph H. Baer and Howard J. Morrison.

Simon lights one of the coloured pads and sounds a tone, then two, then three, etc. Player attempts to match Simon by pressing the pads in the proper sequence. In case of fail player gets a 'fail' sound and is out of the game. Of course Simon speeds up as the game progresses so it becomes increasingly more difficult.
